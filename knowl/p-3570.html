Give an example of an indexed family of sets \(\left\{ A_n \left| n \in \N \right. \right\}\) such all three of the following conditions are true:

  1. For each \(m \in \N\text{,}\) \(A_m \subseteq (0, 1)\text{;}\)

  2. For each \(j, k \in \N\text{,}\) if \(j \ne k\text{,}\) then \(A_j \cap A_k \ne \emptyset\text{;}\) and

  3. \(\bigcap\limits_{k \in \N}^{}A_k = \emptyset\text{.}\)
