The idea of equality of functions has been in the background of our discussion of functions, and it is now time to discuss it explicitly. The preliminary work for this discussion was Beginning Activity 1, in which \(D = \mathbb{N} - \left\{ {1, 2} \right\}\text{,}\) and there were two functions:

  • \(d\x D \to \mathbb{N} \cup \left\{ 0 \right\}\text{,}\) where \(d( n )\) is the number of diagonals of a convex polygon with \(n\) sides

  • \(f\x \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R}\text{,}\) where \(f( x ) = \dfrac{{x\left( {x - 3} \right)}}{2}\text{,}\) for each real number \(x\text{.}\)
