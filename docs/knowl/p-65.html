The first chapter sets the stage for the rest of the book. It introduces students to the use of conditional statements in mathematics, begins instruction in the process of constructing a direct proof of a conditional statement, and introduces many of the writing guidelines that will be used throughout the rest of the book. This is not meant to be a thorough introduction to methods of proof.
