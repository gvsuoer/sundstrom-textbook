    Subsection The Division Algorithm

    Let \(a\) and \(b\) by integers with \(b \gt 0\text{.}\) Then there exist unique integers \(q\) and \(r\) such that

    \begin{equation*} a = bq + r \text{ and } 0 \leq r \lt b\text{.} \end{equation*}
