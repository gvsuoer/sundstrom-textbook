Exercise 6.

(a)

Calculate \(1 + 3 + 5 + \cdots + \left( {2n - 1} \right)\) for several natural numbers \(n\text{.}\)

(b)

Based on your work in Task a, if \(n \in \mathbb{N}\text{,}\) make a conjecture about the value of the sum \(1 + 3 + 5 + \cdots + \left( {2n - 1} \right) = \sum\limits_{j = 1}^n {\left( {2j - 1} \right)}\text{.}\)

(c)

Use mathematical induction to prove your conjecture in Task b.
