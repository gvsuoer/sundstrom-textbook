Answer 4.a.1.

We let \(\beta \in \Lambda\) and let \(x \in A_\beta\text{.}\) Then \(x \in A_\alpha\text{,}\) for at least one \(\alpha \in \Lambda\) and, hence, \(x \in \bigcup\limits_{\alpha \in \Lambda}^{}A_\alpha\text{.}\) This proves that \(A_\beta \subseteq \bigcup\limits_{\alpha \in \Lambda}^{}A_\alpha\text{.}\)
