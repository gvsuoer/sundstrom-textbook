Exercise 4.

Prove Item 2 and Item 4 of Theorem 5.39. Let \(\Lambda\) be a nonempty indexing set and let \(\mathscr{A} = \left\{ A_\alpha \mid \alpha \in \Lambda \right\}\) be an indexed family of sets.

(a)

For each \(\beta \in \Lambda\text{,}\) \(A_\beta \subseteq \bigcup\limits_{\alpha \in \Lambda}^{}A_\alpha\text{.}\)

Answer.
(b)

\(\left(\:\bigcup\limits_{\alpha \in \Lambda}^{}A_\alpha \right)^c = \bigcap\limits_{\alpha \in \Lambda}^{}A_{\alpha}^c\)
