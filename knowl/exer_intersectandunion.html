Exercise 7.

Let \(A\) and \(B\) be subsets of some universal set \(U\text{.}\) Prove each of the following:

(a)

\(A \cap B \subseteq A\)

Answer.
(b)

\(A \subseteq A \cup B\)

Answer.
(c)

\(A \cap A = A\)

(d)

\(A \cup A = A\)

(e)

\(A \cap \emptyset = \emptyset\)

Answer.
(f)

\(A \cup \emptyset = A\)
