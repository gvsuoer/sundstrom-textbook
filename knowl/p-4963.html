Let \(A = \left\{ a, b, c, d, e, f \right\}\text{,}\) and assume that \(\sim\) is an equivalence relation on \(A\text{.}\) Also assume that it is known that

\begin{align*} a \sim b \amp \amp a \not \sim c \amp \amp e \sim f\\ a \sim d \amp \amp a \not \sim f \amp \amp e \not \sim c \end{align*}

Draw a complete directed graph for the equivalence relation \(\sim\) on the set \(A\text{,}\) and then determine all of the equivalence classes for this equivalence relation.
