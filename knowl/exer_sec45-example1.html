Exercise 1.

For each natural number \(n\text{,}\) let \(A_n = \left\{n, n+1, n+2, n+3 \right\}\text{.}\) Use the roster method to specify each of the following sets:

(a)

\(\bigcap\limits_{j=1}^{3}A_j\)

Answer.
(b)

\(\bigcup\limits_{j=1}^{3}A_j\)

(c)

\(\bigcap\limits_{j=3}^{7}A_j\)

(d)

\(\bigcup\limits_{j=3}^{7}A_j\)

Answer.
(e)

\(A_9 \cap \left(\:\bigcup\limits_{j=3}^{7}A_j \right)\)

(f)

\(\bigcup\limits_{j=3}^{7}\left( A_9 \cap A_j \right)\)
