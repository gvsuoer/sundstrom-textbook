In this case, we start with the left side.

\begin{align*} \left[ P \to \left( Q \wedge R \right) \right] \amp \equiv \mynot P \vee \left( Q \wedge R \right)\\ \amp \equiv \left( \mynot P \vee Q \right) \wedge \left( \mynot P \vee R \right)\\ \amp \equiv \left( P \to Q \right) \wedge \left( P \to R \right)\text{.} \end{align*}
