Answer 10.a.1.

The given statement is a conditional statement. We can rewrite the subset relations in terms of conditional sentences: \(A \subseteq B\) means, “For all \(x \in U\text{,}\) if \(x \in A\text{,}\) then \(x \in B\text{,}\)” and \(B^c \subseteq A^c\) means, “For all \(x \in U\text{,}\) if \(x \in B^c\text{,}\) then \(x \in A^c\text{.}\)”
