Determine the values of \(d(3)\text{,}\) \(d(4)\text{,}\) \(d(5)\text{,}\) \(d(6)\text{,}\) \(d(7)\text{,}\) and \(d(8)\text{.}\) Arrange the results in the form of a table of values for the function \(d\text{.}\)
