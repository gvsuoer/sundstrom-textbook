In this case, it may be better to work with the right side first.

\begin{align*} \left( {P \to R} \right) \vee \left( {Q \to R} \right) \amp \equiv \left( \mynot P \vee R \right) \vee \left( \mynot Q \vee R \right)\\ \amp \equiv \left( \mynot P \vee \mynot Q \right) \vee \left( R \vee R \right)\\ \amp \equiv \left( \mynot P \vee \mynot Q \right) \vee R\\ \amp \equiv \mynot \left( P \wedge Q \right) \vee R\\ \amp \equiv \left( P \wedge Q \right) \to R\text{.} \end{align*}
