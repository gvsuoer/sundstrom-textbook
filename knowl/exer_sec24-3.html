Exercise 4.

Write each of the following statements as an English sentence that does not use the symbols for quantifiers.

(a)

\(\left( {\exists m} \in \Z \right)\left( {\exists n} \in \Z \right)\left( {m > n} \right)\)

Answer.
(b)

\(\left( {\exists m} \in \Z \right)\left( {\forall n} \in \Z \right)\left( {m > n} \right)\)

(c)

\(\left( {\forall m} \in \Z \right)\left( {\exists n} \in \Z \right)\left( {m > n} \right)\)

(d)

\(\left( {\forall m}\in \Z \right)\left( {\forall n} \in \Z \right)\left( {m > n} \right)\)

(e)

\(\left( {\exists n} \in \Z \right)\left( {\forall m} \in \Z \right)\left( {m^2 > n} \right)\)

Answer.
(f)

\(\left( {\forall n} \in \Z \right)\left( {\exists m} \in \Z \right)\left( {m^2 > n} \right)\)
