Let \(\R^*\) be the set of nonnegative real numbers, and for each \(r \in \R^*\text{,}\) let

\begin{align*} C_r \amp = \left\{ (x, y) \in \R \times \R \mid x^2 + y^2 = r^2 \right\}\\ D_r \amp = \left\{ (x, y) \in \R \times \R \mid x^2 + y^2 \leq r^2 \right\}\\ T_r \amp = \left\{ (x, y) \in \R \times \R \mid x^2 + y^2 > r^2 \right\} = D_r^c\text{.} \end{align*}

If \(r > 0\text{,}\) then the set \(C_r\) is the circle of radius \(r\) with center at the origin as shown in Figure 5.43, and the set \(D_r\) is the shaded disk (including the boundary) shown in Figure 5.43.
