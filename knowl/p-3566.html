For each natural number \(n\text{,}\) let \(A_n = \left\{ x \in \R \left| \hspace{3pt} n -1 \lt x \lt n \right. \right\}\text{.}\) Prove that \(\left\{ A_n \left| \hspace{3pt} n \in \N \right. \right\}\) is a pairwise disjoint family of sets and that \(\bigcup\limits_{n \in \N}^{}A_n = \left( \R^+ - \N \right)\text{.}\)
