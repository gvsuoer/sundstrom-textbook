For each positive real number \(r\text{,}\) define \(T_r\) to be the closed interval \(\left[ -r^2, r^2 \right]\text{.}\) That is,

\begin{equation*} T_r = \left\{ x \in \R \left| -r^2 \leq x \leq r^2 \right. \right\}\!\text{.} \end{equation*}
