\(P\text{:}\) 15 is odd
\(Q\text{:}\) 15 is prime

(a)

\(P \wedge Q\text{.}\)

(b)

\(P \vee Q\)

(c)

\(P \wedge \mynot Q\)

(d)

\(\mynot P \vee \mynot Q\)
